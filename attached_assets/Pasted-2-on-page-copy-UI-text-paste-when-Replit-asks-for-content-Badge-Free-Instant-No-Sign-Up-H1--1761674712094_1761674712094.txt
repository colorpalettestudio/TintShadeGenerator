H2: What’s the Difference Between Tints and Shades?
Tints are lighter versions of a color (more lightness), while shades are darker versions (less lightness). We generate perceptually even steps so each jump feels consistent.

H2: Supported Color Formats
Paste HEX (#RRGGBB), RGB (rgb(…)), or HSL (hsl(…)). Comma or newline separated. We auto-clean and validate entries.
